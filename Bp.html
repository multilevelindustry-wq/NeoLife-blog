Magnesium for Blood Vessel Relaxation

Magnesium helps blood vessels relax, which supports healthy blood flow and normal blood pressure levels. Many adults are deficient in magnesium due to processed diets and stress. Potassium for Sodium Balance

Potassium works in opposition to sodium. While sodium can raise blood pressure, potassium helps the body excrete excess sodium and eases tension in blood vessel walls. Diets low in fruits and vegetables often lack adequate potassium, which is why nutritional support can be helpful.

Exercise strengthens the heart, improves circulation, and helps maintain a healthy weight. Even brisk walking for 30 minutes a day supports better blood pressure control.
